FFXIV In Brief

Final Fantasy XIV (FFXIV) is a Massively Multiplayer Online Role-Playing Game (MMORPG) developed by Square Enix.

When it originally released in 2010 the game was considered a complete failure and almost bankrupted the company, however due to a number of emergency leadership changes the game was able to be saved and relaunched in 2012 to critical acclaim.
In the years since the game has gone on to become one of the highest grossing MMORPG titles on the market and is highly regarded among the fans as one of the best MMORPG titles to ever release.
